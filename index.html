About Me

My name is Estiven Salazar. I am currently 23 years old, and living in Summit, New Jersey. My family, and I moved to the United States when I was only 5 years old from Costa Rica. Upon graduating from high school I have worked in many job fields ranging from a full-time office sales position to assistant-manager of the Purchasing, Warehouse, and Logistics Departments for a small importer, and distributor of Specialty Foods. As well as serving, and bartender at several restaurants.

Contact Me

Working in all of these different fields has taught me the discipline, and humility that I believe will assist me in overcoming the many adversities yet to come. I have recently chosen to pursue a career in Web Development, and it all began just out of curiosity as to how the internet works, and what the term "coding" really meant in a professional setting. Once I began to research these curiosities, I then became enthralled in the world of Web Development, and started trying to learn several languages on my own using the internet itself. After a few months I began to hear of Coding Bootcamps, soon enough I was extremely fortunate to begin the application process, and get accepted into the Rutgers University Coding Bootcamp! I am unbelieveably excited to begin this process, and become a Full-Stack Web Developer!
